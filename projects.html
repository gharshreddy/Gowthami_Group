SV NAGAR

SRI SV NAGAR, conceptualized to bring in together the culture and tradition of Vizianagaram, the trend and buzz of Visakhapatnam. Therefore, one can call it a twin city. It is located near the Ramanarayanam Theme Park on Korukonda road, Situated among many premium gated communities. It's 5 minutes ride from the Vizianagaram railway station and bus terminus, less than 25 minutes journey to Bhogapuram International Airport and 30 minutes drive to Visakhapatnam urban. Famous schools like Korukonda Sainik school and the Vizag International schools, some of the best colleges, hotels, restaurants, cinema theatres, shopping malls and hospitals are very nearby.
